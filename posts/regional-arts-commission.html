June 24, 2022 • Board Bill 66

Regional Arts Commission Funding

This ordinance appropriated $10.6 million in American Rescue Plan Act (ARPA) funds to support arts and cultural institutions through the Regional Arts Commission. The funding was divided into three grant programs: General Operating Support ($4M), Program Support ($3.3M), and Artist Support ($3.3M). This investment addressed the severe economic impact of the pandemic on the arts sector, which welcomes 12 million visitors annually and generates $600 million in economic activity while supporting 19,000 jobs in the St. Louis region.

June 3, 2022 • Board Bill 44

Charter Commission

This charter amendment established a decennial Charter Commission to review and propose amendments to the City Charter. The commission consists of nine members recommended by alderpersons, nominated by the Mayor, and confirmed by the Board of Aldermen. The commission is designed to ensure diverse representation across race, gender, socioeconomic status, geography, and age. Modeled after the Ferguson Commission's inclusive approach, the process emphasizes public engagement, transparency, and expert consultation. The commission solicits public input, publishes draft amendments, and holds public hearings before submitting proposed changes to voters for approval by three-fifths vote.

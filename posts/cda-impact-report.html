CDA Impact Report 2020-2024

December 2024

Empowering, Developing & Transforming St. Louis Neighborhoods - A comprehensive review of the Community Development Administration's investments and impact from 2020-2024.

Download Report (PDF)

Overview

The CDA Impact Report 2020-2024 showcases the transformative work of the City of St. Louis Community Development Administration over a five-year period. As Executive Director from September 2022 onwards, I led efforts that tripled the agency's investments while maintaining zero audit findings.

Key Achievements

  • $246 million committed to economic justice initiatives
  • 3,624 affordable housing units in development pipeline
  • Over 1,000 home repairs completed in 2024 alone
  • $150 million in ARPA funds allocated to North City revitalization

Strategic Pillars

The report details CDA's comprehensive approach through three strategic pillars:

  • Economic Empowerment - Business support, workforce development, and educational justice initiatives totaling $61 million
  • Equitable & Inclusive Development - Incentive reform, site assembly, and commercial corridor revitalization totaling $77 million
  • Neighborhood Transformation - Capacity building, vacancy reduction, and housing accessibility totaling $108 million

Community Impact

This report demonstrates how strategic public investment can catalyze neighborhood transformation. Through partnerships with developers, nonprofits, and community organizations, CDA has:

  • Expanded access to affordable housing across qualified census tracts
  • Supported small businesses through grants and technical assistance
  • Invested in digital equity with free public Wi-Fi in parks and corridors
  • Funded early childhood education centers and health facilities
  • Transformed vacant properties into community assets

Looking Forward

The CDA Impact Report establishes a foundation for continued equitable development in St. Louis. It reflects a commitment to transparency, community engagement, and data-driven decision making that will guide future investments in our neighborhoods.
